PRODUCT 01 Laterite

Laterite — Cement-Grade, Karnataka Origin

Tierra Milestone's laterite is extracted from controlled Karnataka deposits with consistent Al₂O₃ and Fe₂O₃ ratios suited to cement raw mix correction and brick manufacturing. Each batch is sampled at the pit and certified before dispatch.

PRODUCT 02 Clay

Clay — Kiln-Feed Grade, Low-Silica

Our clay is sourced from Karnataka deposits characterised by stable plasticity and low contamination, making it ideal for cement raw mix correction and tile/brick manufacturing. Moisture is controlled at dispatch to prevent handling and kiln-feed issues.

ParameterSpecification (Typical Range)
