OUR STORY

Why Tierra Milestone Exists

Cement plants in southern India operate on razor-thin tolerance for supply disruption. A single unplanned kiln stoppage costs lakhs per hour. Yet the raw material supply chain — laterite, clay, iron ore, bauxite — has long been managed by informal, unregistered vendors with no documentation, no consistency, and no accountability.

Tierra Milestone was established to close that gap. We are a Karnataka-registered mining company operating under full MMDR Act compliance, with NABL-accredited quality testing, documented logistics partnerships, and a founding team that has spent years in industrial supply chain management.

Our focus is singular: be the upstream partner that procurement heads can stake their supply security on — with documentation that survives an audit, quality that performs in the kiln, and logistics that don't flinch at monsoon season.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Five Operating Values

These are not aspirational statements. They are the commitments against which we measure every shipment, every certification, and every client interaction.

📋

Compliance First

We operate above the minimum. Every lease, every clearance, every test result is documented and available to customers on request. No exceptions. No informal workarounds.

🎯

Specification Integrity

What we quote in the spec sheet is what arrives in the truck. Grade consistency is non-negotiable — we batch-test and certify every consignment before dispatch.

⏱️

Logistics Reliability

A minerals supplier is only as valuable as their ability to deliver on schedule. Our logistics network is built around plant operations calendars, not vendor convenience.

🌱

Environmental Responsibility

Karnataka's mining regulations exist for good reason. We hold ourselves to EC compliance, progressive mine rehabilitation, and dust/runoff control standards that go beyond the legal minimum.

🤝

Long-Term Partnership

We build supply relationships, not transactions. Procurement partner agreements, volume commitments, and price stability windows are designed to make us a genuine extension of our clients' supply chain.
