REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

Every Permit. Every Clearance. Every Payment.

Our compliance stack covers every statutory obligation from mining lease through to transport documentation. Your vendor audit is a one-hour exercise, not a three-week investigation.

✅

Mining Leases — Karnataka MMDR

Valid Karnataka State mining leases and quarry permissions under the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act. Applications tracked and renewed proactively before expiry.

✅

Environmental Clearance

EC obtained under the EIA Notification (2006) for applicable operations. Environmental Management Plan (EMP) active with progressive reclamation obligations.

✅

KSPCB Consent to Operate

Consent to Establish and Consent to Operate current under Karnataka State Pollution Control Board norms for air and water quality management.

✅

Royalty, DMF & NMET Payments

All royalty, District Mineral Foundation (DMF), and National Mineral Exploration Trust (NMET) levies paid and recorded per mineral and quarter. No arrears.

✅

IBM Registration & Reporting

Registered with the Indian Bureau of Mines. Monthly and annual production and sales returns filed on schedule under MCDR provisions.

✅

GST & Tax Compliance

GST registration current. All transactions issued with compliant tax invoices. Correct HSN codes applied by mineral type. TDS obligations met.

✅

Labour Compliance — EPF / ESIC

EPF and ESIC registrations active. Contractor labour engaged under registered agreements with proper compliance verification. Minimum wage compliance confirmed.

✅

Transport Documentation

All consignments carry valid transport permits, delivery challans, and royalty-paid waybills. Chain-of-custody from pit to plant documented per batch.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Our Quality System Isn't a PDF We Show at Audits.
It's How We Work Every Day.

Closed-loop quality control from pit sampling to batch release, with NABL-accredited external laboratory confirmation on every consignment.

🔬

Inbound Lot Sampling

Every incoming load sampled using standardised cone-and-quartering protocol. Sample retained for 90 days post-dispatch for dispute resolution.

⚡

Field Rapid Test

On-site moisture analyser and particle-size sieve on every batch. Rejected batches quarantined immediately — never blended forward to fill an order.

🏅

NABL Lab Confirmation

Chemical parameters (Al₂O₃, Fe₂O₃, SiO₂, LOI, etc.) confirmed at ISO/IEC 17025-accredited external laboratory before stock release for dispatch.

🔗

Batch Traceability

Every lot assigned a unique batch ID linking GPS origin tag, supplier, extraction date, QA results, and dispatch record. Full chain-of-custody from pit to gate.

📄

Material Test Certificate

Issued per consignment. Delivered digitally within 24 hours of material arrival at your plant. Ready for your QC file — no chasing required.

🎯

Non-Conformance SLA

Quality complaint acknowledged within 4 hours. Root-cause analysis completed within 48 hours. Corrective-action report issued within 7 days — documented and shared.

🏆

ISO 9001 Certification — In Progress

We are currently advancing ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification. Target accreditation: within 12–18 months of commercial operations launch. All current QA processes are aligned to ISO 9001 framework requirements.

ENVIRONMENTAL & SOCIAL

Mines Operated with Community Trust
and Environmental Accountability.

Our ESG commitments are operational, not aspirational. Every mine site operates under an active Environmental Management Plan with measurable obligations.

🌱

Progressive Mine Reclamation

Mined-out zones backfilled and revegetated per approved Mine Closure Plan. Rehabilitation bonds funded and maintained. Phased reclamation occurs concurrently with extraction — not deferred to closure.

💧

Water & Air Quality

Active dust suppression across all processing yards, covered stockpiles for moisture-sensitive material, and drainage-compliant yard surfaces. KSPCB ambient air and water monitoring ongoing.

👷

Community Employment

Local Karnataka workforce preference for site operations, transport contracts, and auxiliary services. Community grievance mechanism in place with documented resolution protocols.

🦺

Safety & Occupational Health

PPE mandatory on all sites with no exceptions. Certified Mines Manager on record per statutory requirement. Monthly safety drills and occupational health check-ups for all permanent site personnel.

🔍

Responsible Sourcing

Supplier qualification process includes statutory compliance verification for all third-party miners. Unregistered or permit-lapsed miners are excluded from our supply chain — no exceptions for price.

🌿

Environmental Management Plan

Site-specific EMP active at all operational areas. Covers topsoil preservation, drainage design, noise monitoring, and progressive rehabilitation milestones with mandatory reporting to state authorities.

ALL-SEASON OPERATIONS

We Plan for Monsoon.
So You Don't Have To.

Karnataka monsoon is factored into every supply contract — not used as an excuse for missed deliveries. Pre-monsoon buffer builds, covered storage, and drainage-engineered yards ensure supply continuity June through September.
