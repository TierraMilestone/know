PARTNER PROGRAM

Apply for the Procurement Partner Program

Our Procurement Partner Program is an invitation-only framework for cement plants and industrial manufacturers seeking a contracted, specification-guaranteed supply relationship.

Partner Program Benefits

  • ⚡ Priority Allocation — First access to certified stock during supply-tight periods
  • 🔒 Locked Pricing Windows — 3–12 month price-stability agreements
  • 📊 Quarterly Supply Reviews — Joint QA and logistics planning sessions
  • 💵 Volume Rebate Programme — Quarterly rebates for exceeding committed tonnage
  • 🚨 48-Hour Emergency SLA — Emergency dispatch for contracted accounts

Who Should Apply?

  • ✓ Cement plants consuming 2,000+ MT/month
  • ✓ Industrial manufacturers needing documented supplier relationships
  • ✓ Procurement teams replacing unreliable spot-market vendors
  • ✓ Plants planning capacity expansion requiring increased raw material sourcing
