HOW WE DELIVER

The 6-Step Pit-to-Plant Delivery Model

Every shipment follows the same auditable workflow. Procurement officers can track every step — and hold us accountable at every stage.

1

Extraction

Controlled pit mining at Karnataka leased deposits. Daily production logs and statutory safety compliance.

2

On-Site Sampling

Every load sampled using standardised cone-and-quartering protocol. GPS-tagged per truck at reception.

3

QA Lab Testing

Rapid field test for moisture and particle size, then confirmatory assay at NABL-accredited external lab.

4

Certification

Released lot assigned batch certificate. Only certified stock dispatched — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

5

GPS Dispatch

Contracted tipper/bulk truck fleet. Weighbridge certificate issued. Shipment GPS-tracked to plant gate.

6

Delivery & Docs

MTC, delivery challan, transport permit, and GST invoice delivered digitally within 24 hours of arrival.
